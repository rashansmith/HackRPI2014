Connecting Girls & Women in Computer Science all Around The World

sheConnect is an online platform that brings all resources related to women and girls in Computer Science in one place. As a minorty is Computer Science there is still a need for support not only for women to enter these fields, but also to stay. As a result, sheConnect aims to bring these ambitious women together by providing them will all information related to opportunities, scholarships, and events.
